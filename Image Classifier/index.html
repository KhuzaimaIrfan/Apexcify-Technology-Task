Face Expression Classifier

Real-time emotion detection using Teachable Machine

Click "Start" to enable webcam

How it Works

1. Webcam Input

Your browser captures video frames from your webcam in real-time.

2. TensorFlow.js

The frames are processed locally in your browser using a Deep Learning model.

3. Classification

The model compares the input against "Happy", "Sad", and "Neutral" patterns to predict your expression.
